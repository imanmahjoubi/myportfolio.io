Nao Control

This a multi-platform app developed within my school .

Nao Control is a multi-platform app (Android, Ios, and Windows Phone) that allows you to control the robot Nao from your tablet, you only need to connect to wifi and turn your robot on.

With this app you can let the robot:

  • Stand up, sit down, walk, shake hands..
  • Dance
  • Say Text written on the tablet

  • year: 2015
  • Developed by: Imen Mahjoubi, Yassin Sebai, Maroua Ben Gamra
  • Category: iOT, Mobile
